## Hey, I'm Saurabh 👋

I'm a Security Engineer with a focus on cloud security, application security, and bug bounty research. I spend most of my time thinking about how systems break — and occasionally writing about it here.

This blog is where I put the things that don't fit neatly into a Jira ticket: attack walkthroughs, lessons from bug bounty programs, AWS misconfigurations I've found in the wild, and the occasional career reflection about what "senior" actually means in security.

## What I write about

- **Cloud Security** — AWS misconfigurations, IAM rabbit holes, Cognito pitfalls, and the specific ways cloud-native architectures create new attack surfaces
- **Bug Bounty** — Recon methodology, responsible disclosure, and real findings with enough detail to actually be useful
- **Security Fundamentals** — The principles that matter more than any tool — least privilege, defense in depth, fail-secure design
- **AI & Productivity** — How security engineers can use LLMs without giving away the farm
- **Career** — The parts of senior security work that no certification prepares you for

## Background

I've been in security long enough to have made most of the classic mistakes myself. I hold the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner certification and actively participate in bug bounty programs. My day-to-day involves a mix of cloud security architecture, penetration testing, and the kind of judgment calls that have no StackOverflow answers.

Outside of work: building tools, writing for Medium, and trying to get better at explaining complex security concepts to people who don't live in the terminal.
